The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Bangladesh envisions a future of prosperity, innovation. With its rapidly growing economy, the nation aspires to become a global leader in technology, education, and sustainable development. Investments in renewable energy, modern infrastructure, and digital transformation pave the way for a brighter tomorrow. The youth, being the country's most valuable asset, are driving progress through innovation, entrepreneurship, and creativity. Social equity and environmental stewardship remain at the forefront, ensuring no one is left behind. From rural communities to urban centers, Bangladesh is determined to overcome challenges and embrace a future where every citizen thrives in harmony with nature and technology.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
